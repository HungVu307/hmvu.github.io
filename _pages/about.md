Hung Vu is currently an Applied Scientist at NVIDIA (Vietnam). Prior to that, he spent a year as an AI Engineer at VinBigdata (VinGroup). He graduated with honors in Automation and Control Engineering from Hanoi University of Science and Technology, where he was mentored by Prof. Truong Pham and Prof. Thao Tran at IPSAL.

His research focuses on Vision Language Models, Computer Vision, and Signal Processing.
